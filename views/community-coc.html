Community Code of Conduct

Here at Gochí, we require you to follow all of our regulations and guidelines. Bypassing or finding numerous loopholes to any of these regulations will result in a severe consequence. Any questions regarding these regulations and guidelines should be directed to a Human Resources Department member. Please be sure to abide all regulations and guidelines listed below.

✧ RESPECT

At Gochí, we value respect as it is an extremely important key aspect to the group. When communicating in the Gochí game facilities and communications server, always be respectful to your peers, and staff. Any form of disrespect is not tolerated and will result in consequences.

✧ APPROPRIATENESS

When on any platforms that the Gochí community uses, it's required you remain appropriate. This includes any discussions and debates that may come up. A key value of Gochí is being an open community for everyone. Upon joining Gochí, you are agreeing to remain appropriate at all times. Failure to comply with this rule will result in consequences.

✧ DISRUPTION

Here at Gochí, our goal is to satisfy our community! Acting inappropriately, or hacking on our premises is strictly prohibited. Also known as trolling and exploiting. A few examples of trolling may be: bothering others, ordering improperly, being disrespectful, and so on. Failure to abide the rules will result in a consequence. Consequences vary based on the situation.

✧ DRESS CODE

Here at Gochí, we have a specific dress code that our community and staff need to adhere. Staff members are not permitted to wear any troll outfits, inappropriate outfits, or outfits with inappropriate sayings. All shirts must be respectful at all times. As a community member, you are also not permitted to wear any inappropriate outfits or outfits with inappropriate sayings. Ranks Management and higher have stricter dress code that they need to follow. Failure to comply with this rule of conduct will result in a server consequence.

✧ DEROGATORY OR DISCRIMINATING SPEECH

Within all Gochí facilities and our communications server, discrimination is strictly prohibited and will not be tolerated under any circumstance. Stating derogatory terms or discriminating against an individual(s) on the bases of race, color, religion, gender, national origin, & disability is not allowed. Gochí’s community and our facilities are a safe space for all individuals and action will be taken if this rule is not followed. Failure to comply with this rule will result in major consequences.

✧ COMMON SENSE

Here at Gochí, we expect our workers to be at their best at all times. With that being said, we expect them to use their common sense within Gochí’s facilities. Common sense is the basic level of practical knowledge and judgment that we all need in order for our workplace to run smoothly. If we have noticed that you are failing to integrate common sense to your work ethic, consequences will be given.

✧ VOICE CHANNEL REGULATIONS

Within any voice channels in Gochí’s communications server, you are expected to remain respectful at all times towards other individuals and follow our Code of Conduct. You are not permitted to cause disruptive/excessive noise or state any inappropriate words while speaking in a voice channel. Additionally, you are not permitted to sing outside of karaoke events unless permission from every other individual within that specific channel has granted permission for you to do so.

✧ DRAMA

Gochí has a strict no-drama rule at all of our facilities. Any disrespect or rude/hate speech directed towards other members in the server will result in immediate punishment. If you have drama with others in the server, please direct the problem to DMs and attempt to resolve it in DMs instead of our server.
