Appeal Information

This page contains information regarding appealing information for both ROBLOX and Discord. If you are banned in either one, please use the appropriate form to request an appeal.

Information Regarding Bans

Game bans are issued for users who were disrupting the user experience at our game(s). Discord bans are for those who have violated our regulations and are subject to punishment. If you believe you should be unbanned, please fill out an appeal here.

Information Regarding Blacklists

If you were blacklisted from working with us as being part of our management team, you are able to appeal your blacklist with a good amount of proof to prove your case of irreproachablity here.
