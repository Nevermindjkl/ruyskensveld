Frequently Asked Questions

These are the questions that are frequently asked by our community members. If your question is not here, feel free to DM one of our executives!

  • What is Korean BBQ?

    Korean BBQ is a mainstream option of group dining where you and your party members bond, grilling and feasting upon countless waves of marinated meat and other trendy Korean dishes. Enjoy the exhilaration of self-grilling with your peers, and you will conclude the experience with an awareness of the significance a Korean BBQ has to offer.

  • How do I become an employee?

    To become a staff member at Gochí, visit our application center, located on our group page! A submitted application will take up to 24 hours to be read by our Management Team+.

  • How do I handle a disruptive individual?

    Communications server: Screenshot evidence ➙ log the situation, following the format, in the #restaurant-assistance channel located in our Discord server. Restaurant: Warn the individual ➙ contact a Restaurant Supervisor+ to ban. If it's an exploiter, contact a Restaurant Supervisor+ to ban.

  • Are there management applications?

    Once our facilities are released, management applications will release as well. Ensure to join our communications server to obtain the opportunity to apply.

  • Can I form an alliance with Gochí?

    The alliance guide and application is managed by our Public Relations Department. The alliance section of our handbook is located above the FAQ section.
