Rank Information & Limits

Information regarding ranks and their limits. We've also added descriptions so you know what you're doing.

CUSTOMER - UNLIMITED

Individuals who have joined the group are a part of our community. They can eat at the restaurant, vibe in our lounges, and enjoy the KBBQ experience.

NOTED CUSTOMER - UNLIMITED

Individuals that have contributed to Gochí. This includes nitro boosting, being a former Executive Team member, or being well known within the community.

TRAINEE [LR] - UNLIMITED

Individuals who applied for a position to join our staff team. Trainees are expected to attend a training session before getting promoted to the next rank. Trainees are not allowed to work until they have successfully passed a training session.

SERVING STAFF [LR] - UNLIMITED

This is the first employed rank of the staff team. Serving Staff start off at the front desk and claim tables for their guests. After seating the party member(s), they take their orders with the given staff tablet. They must collect the items from the kitchen and give them to the party member(s). Serving Staff should periodically check on the table to verify that the guests are doing well. They must obtain 50 points at the restaurant to be eligible to return to the Training Center, and train for the rank of Kitchen Staff.

KITCHEN STAFF [LR] - UNLIMITED

This is the second employed rank of the staff team. They are in charge of preparing all food and drink items that the party member(s) order. Kitchen Staff are permitted to stay in the kitchen and receive orders whilst occasionally obtaining information regarding orders via. the ticket system. They are to restock the ingredients whenever the stock is low. They must obtain 100 points at the restaurant to be eligible to return to the Training Center, and train for the rank of Senior Staff.

SENIOR STAFF [LR] - UNLIMITED

This is the final staff position that would be able to work towards the rank of Restaurant Assistant. They are to help out the Serving Staff with hosting tables and delivering the courses, and prepare and restock items in the kitchen alongside the Kitchen Staff. They should be role models to the lower staff ranks, and be taking over the positions that have low staff operating them.

RESTAURANT ASSISTANT [MR] - 40

These individuals are those who have successfully passed their internship after getting in through public applications or recommendations. Given this position and its ability, it enables them to train individuals. Like any other rank, they may supervise and ensure the restaurant is secure from any irresponsible players.

RESTAURANT SUPERVISOR [MR] - 30

These individuals are those who have obtained this rank have proved their dedication to Gochí. Restaurant Supervisors are able to train, and are the first management position to be able to host training sessions. They are expected to set a good example for others.

KITCHEN MANAGER [MR] - 20

These individuals have the same abilities as all the other management members below their rank. Kitchen Managers are also required to be in the kitchen when supervising the restaurant to make sure everything is running smoothly. They play a valuable role model for all community members and positions lower than them, as they are progressing closer to the Executive Team.

GENERAL MANAGER [MR] - 15

These individuals are in charge of doing everything else Middle Ranks are tasked with. General Manager is the last management rank and should represent their group in a good manner. They should also have good reputations, maintain great activity, and communicate strongly with the community. They are eligible to become executives if they exceed beyond their expectations.

EXECUTIVE ASSISTANT [HR] - 12

This is the first High Rank. Additionally, it is the first rank that is required to be placed in a department, Human Resources or Public Relations. They are expected to participate in their department tasks and activities. This rank may oversee in sessions, and supervise at the restaurant. They will respond to group inquiries and follow their given responsibilities.

CHIEF PUBLIC RELATIONS OFFICER [HR] - 3

After Executive Assistant, an individual in the Public Relations Department may receive a promotion to Chief Public Relations Officer. They are to lead the Public Relations Department alongside their head. These individuals have shown exceptional leadership and collaborative skills and their responsibility is to guide and help their fellow department members if needed. Chief Public Relations Officers work to plan events, form and maintain alliances, and work on any of the other regular Public Relations tasks.

CHIEF HUMAN RESOURCES OFFICER [HR] - 3

After Executive Assistant, an individual in the Human Resources Department may receive a promotion to Chief Human Resources Officer. They are to lead the Human Resources Department alongside their head. These individuals have shown exceptional leadership and collaborative skills and are more adept when it comes to staff-related tasks, including but not limited to staff employment, promotions, and more. Chief Human Resources Officers all work together to ensure smooth operations within their department.

CHIEF ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICER [HR] - 2

This is the final position within the Executive Team. The Chief Administrative Officers work with the Presidential Board to lead the two departments. They will be managing their respective departments, and the resources of those departments. They'll also be the main point of communication between both departments to the heads.

PRESIDENTIAL BOARD [SR] - 4

These individuals are the Heads of either Human Resources, Public Relations, Moderation, and Marketing & Development. They are in charge of controlling all of the group's internal and external operations, alongside overseeing them. The Presidential Board works with the Chairman and Vice Chairman to ensure prosperity within Gochí.

VICE CHAIRMAN [SR] - 1

The co-owner of the group, and handles all main operations relating to procedures, functions, and development, working closely alongside the Chairman and the rest of the Leadership Team. The Vice Chairman collaborates together with Leadership to handle the departments, financial operations, and other important duties which are crucial to the growth and performance of the group.

CHAIRMAN [SR] - 1

The owner of the group, and handles all main operations relating to procedures, functions, and development. The Chairman works hand-in-hand with the Leadership Team to handle the departments, financial tasks, and other important duties which are crucial to the smooth operation of the group.
