Middle Rank Achievement Guide

Adorations, Senior Staff! As many of you are interested in pursuing a career as a Middle Rank here at Gochí, a promotion guide to aid you throughout your journey has been created. Please note that you must be able to meet a few requirements before receiving an application for Middle Rank. As a Lower Rank, you can achieve Restaurant Assistant by passing applications or receiving recommendations by the Human Resources Department. Below is a list of regulations and guidelines that you must follow in order to achieve Middle Rank.

REQUIREMENTS

The Human Resources Department at Gochí seeks out many important qualities in our Senior Staff. To be recommended and receive the Restaurant Assistant Application, here are the requirements which you must meet.

  • Must be at least 13 years of age or older.
  • Must be in our communications server.
  • Must be dedicated and active in the Gochí community.
  • Must be a Senior Staff in order to receive an application.
  • Must not have any bad history with Gochí or any other group.
  • Must be capable of adequate grammar and proper spelling.
  • Must not have safe-chat while employed as a Middle Rank.
  • Must be a Senior Staff for a minimum of one week before being eligible for recommendation.

RECOMMENDATION TIPS

  • We recommend that you stay active and dedicated to Gochí by working at the restaurant often and engaging with others.
  • Setting a good example and being a leader for other Low Ranks is a wonderful quality that we look for in eligible Senior Staff.
  • Being kind and respectful to others is crucial to being recommended by a High Rank.
  • Having proper spelling, grammar, and punctuation, and an expansive knowledge of English vocabulary is recommended.
  • Do not hint to a Middle Rank+ as this will only decrease your chance of promotion. This includes saying that you are working hard or that you want a promotion.
  • We recommend engaging with the community in the communications server as well to increase your chances.
