Alliance Guide

This contains all the vital information for forming an alliance with Gochí! We can't wait for you to join our family!

Introduction

Greetings! On behalf of Gochí’s Public Relations Department, we are glad that you have shown interest in forming an affiliation with us. Before filling in the application, please make sure that you meet all of our criteria. If you do not meet the criteria and send the application, it will automatically be declined.

Alliance Standards

On behalf of the Public Relations Department, we are always looking for groups to form alliances with. If your group meets the standards, feel free to send an Executive Assistant of Public Relations or anyone in Public Relations a Direct Message on Discord with your group information. Our standards will be listed below.

1. Must have at least 5000 ROBLOX members.

2. Must have at least 700 Discord members.

3. Must provide a welcoming community for all members.

4. Your group must have a good reputation.

5. Your group cannot sell any admin permissions or medium ranks.

6. Your group games and Discord Server must remain active.

7. We do accept exceptions for groups that are below the Discord and Roblox member count.

Alliance Expectations

The Public Relations Department expects your group to meet a list of expectations in order to ally with Gochí. If you have any further questions on the expectations, ensure to contact an Executive Assistant+ in the Public Relations Department.

1. Your group must show moderate activity in the Discord server as well as the group. Your group must grow steadily and have the potential to benefit both your group and Gochí.

2. As a part of our alliances, you are expected to announce our events once informed.

3. We expect that our alliances’ community maintains a professional, mature, and respectful behavior when it comes to entering any Gochí premises such as the restaurant, Discord server, group wall, and Training Center.

4. Alliances are expected to write a constructive response to each of our alliance responses. It is expected that applications are to be put on a Google Document and that the responses are detailed, concise, and professional when it comes to submitting the application.

Alliance Questions

If your group wishes to form an alliance with Gochí, the questions can be found below for you to answer. Before applying, please ensure that your group has met our group standards. As stated previously, we kindly ask that the application is to be sent and formed on a Google Document.

1. What is the name of your establishment? Provide your Discord and group link.

2. Why do you want to ally with Gochí?

3. Why should Gochí ally with your group?

4. How could your group benefit us?

5. Whom are some representatives that we could contact regarding your alliance application status?

6. Do you understand that Gochí has the right to terminate your group if your group violates our requirements and standards?

7. Do you understand that your representatives must inform us of any major changes within your group?

Conclusion

On behalf of the Gochí Public Relations Department, we hope you take the time to consider affiliating with us. If you plan on doing so, we wish you the best of luck on completing your application. As stated beforehand, please make sure to provide your application on a Google Document. Any further inquiries you have about completing any part of the application may be directed to any member of the Public Relations Department.
