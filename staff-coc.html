Staff Code of Conduct

At Gochí, we require all staff members to abide by the Staff Code of Conduct. It is extremely important that you understand and obey the conduct as it is a huge aspect within the group. Failure to comply with the protocols listed below will result in consequences. If you have any questions regarding these regulations and guidelines, please direct them to a member of the Human Resources Department.

✧ RESPECT

Respect and professionalism are highly valued here at Gochí, it is required that you respect yourself and those around you.

✧ INCLUSIVENESS AND DRAMA

Here at Gochí, everyone should be included. Try to exceed our expectations by including others in on discussion or conversations! Additionally, drama is prohibited, and should not be discussed on our premises.

✧ HINTING

Hinting is strictly prohibited here at Gochí, the Management Team has full authority over the consequences you are given due to hinting.

✧ GRAMMAR

Grammar is strictly enforced while working on the Staff Team here at Gochí. Refusal to utilize or lack of grammar will result in a demotion from our Staff Team promptly. Grammar is required at all times, even when you are not working.

✧ PROFESSIONALISM

While working, please make sure to stay on task instead of wandering around. You are allowed to take short breaks, but do not take prolonged breaks unless given permission by an MR+, especially when we are busy.

✧ PROCEDURES

1. In a serious instance of not adhering to these conducts, you will be reminded formally by a Management Team+ member. This doesn’t affect you that much but if you continue not to adhere to these conducts, you will be given the next consequence.

2. A warning will be given to individuals who refuse to adhere to the Staff Code of Conduct for the second time. This will now somewhat affect your career at Gochí because if you still continue to not adhere to these conduct, you will be given the next and final consequence.

3. A termination, and followed by a possible blacklist will be given to individuals who received a warning but still continue to not adhere to the Staff Code of Conduct.
